District Tree

District Tree is an interactive map of street trees in Washington, D.C. It is built in Ruby on Rails on the backend, and React.js on the frontend.

TECHNOLOGIES — Ruby on Rails, PostGIS, React, Leaflet, Mapbox, Semantic UI
