GapGap

GapGap (گپ گپ) is an Urdu expression that means 'to chit-chat'. It's also a chat app that allows its users to have multiple conversations, and create and/or add simple bots. Some of the most popular bots are Elevator Boy, who responds with an elevator code, or Jerry, who cracks your favorite dad joke to brighten your day. GapGap is built in Ruby on Rails and ActionCable on the backend, and React Redux on the frontend.
NOTE: This app is still work in progress.

TECHNOLOGIES — Ruby on Rails, ActionCable, React, Redux, Semantic UI
